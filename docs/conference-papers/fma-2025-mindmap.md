## Main Themes

### 1. Disaster Adaptation Plans (DAPs)
Strategic place-based mechanisms for proactive disaster planning under the NSW Reconstruction Authority Act 2022

- **Core Objectives** — Reduce disaster impacts, increase community resilience, guide proactive investment
- **Regional Approach** — Northern Rivers (multi-hazard) and Hawkesbury-Nepean Valley (flood focus) DAPs, with cross-boundary management
- **Key Tools** — Adaptation Pathways, Risk Profiles, and Project Response Indicators (PRIs)

### 2. Modelling and Analysis
Advanced hydrological modelling, multi-hazard assessment, and climate change impact integration

- **Continuous Simulation Modelling** — URBS model with RILM, sub-daily rainfall data, and 64-bit processing improvements
- **Multi-Hazard Assessment** — Spatial co-location, temporal coincidence, and cascading impact analysis
- **Climate Change Impacts** — Sea level rise projections, Vertical Land Movement (VLM), updated ARR guidance

### 3. Infrastructure and Land Planning
Risk-based planning frameworks for transport infrastructure and urban development

- **Linear Infrastructure** — Road/rail immunity standards, causeway vs. culvert design trade-offs, closure cost impacts
- **Road Safety and Signage** — Flood depth markers, public behaviour campaigns, designated floodways
- **Sub-regional Planning** — Developable area assessment, floodplain storage loss, urban expansion constraints

### 4. Governance and Programs
State and regional governance frameworks for floodplain management

- **NSW Reconstruction Authority (RA)** — Lead agency for DAP implementation
- **Floodplain Management Program (DCCEEW)** — State funding and technical guidance
- **Coastal Management Framework** — Integration of coastal and fluvial flood risk
- **Melbourne Water Flood Program** — Interstate governance model
