### 🌟 Core Paper Matching Your Context (Property-Based Floodplain Management)

**1. Purchase or Move? Lessons Learnt from Historic Property-Based Floodplain Management Schemes**
*   **All Authors:** Alana Mosely
*   **Year Published:** 2020
*   **Key Topic:** Property-based floodplain management. This paper directly addresses the main themes of your query:
    *   **Scheme Types:** Evaluates voluntary and compulsory house purchases, land-swaps, and house resilience (wet/dry flood-proofing and house raising) schemes [1-6].
    *   **Eligibility Criteria:** Analyzes criteria used to determine property eligibility, such as repetitive historical flooding losses, location within high hazard/life-threatening areas, and the necessary relaxing of criteria to prevent splitting up families or socially impacting neighbors [7-11].
    *   **Success Factors:** Highlights sound community engagement, strong local leadership, giving homeowners a variety of options, utilizing the momentum of recent disasters, and enabling tight-knit communities to remain together as key factors for success [11-14].
    *   **Lessons Learnt:** Notes the importance of keeping eligibility criteria simple, the emotional toll and difficulty of relocations for the elderly, the necessity of establishing an income-generating or useful purpose for vacated lands, and the challenges posed by long project timeframes, safety/looting issues, and rising local housing prices [12-15].
